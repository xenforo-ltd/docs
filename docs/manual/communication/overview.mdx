# Communicating with your users

XenForo provides you with several ways to communicate with your users, ranging from on-page, attention-grabbing notices, to email tools for individual users.

These user-communication methods include

- [On-screen notices](notices.mdx)
- [Help pages](help.mdx)
- [Contacting your users directly](contact.mdx)
- [Activity summary email](activity-summary.mdx)
